Why Sponsor Us?

Help students build real engineering judgment.

Sponsoring WE Bots is an investment in future engineers, programmers, designers, problem solvers, and technical leaders.

01Student Development

Members gain practical experience in design, manufacturing, electronics, software, project management, communication, and teamwork.

02Visibility

Sponsors can be recognized on our website, social media, team apparel, project displays, outreach materials, and presentations.

03Purchasing Materials

Funding helps purchase the components, tools, and supplies needed to move Project JOSH beyond concepts and into tested hardware.

04Build the Community

Support helps us run workshops, recruit new members, host technical training, and make robotics more accessible at Western.
